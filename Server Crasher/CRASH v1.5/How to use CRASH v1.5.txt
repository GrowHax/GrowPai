How to use CRASH v1.5 by Yuhkil?

1. Put the script in the Lua Executor of GrowPai and execute the script

2. While in a world type in /c

3. You'll now see a text pop up in your chat saying that the crash has started

4. When you think world is frozen, or you are about to disconnect. Just simply press stop on the executor.

5. Enjoy Crashing
